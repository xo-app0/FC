Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)

How it works

Medication (SSRIs)
Hover to learn more
How it works
SSRIs increase serotonin in the brain, reducing the intensity of the fear response. Benzodiazepines may be used short-term for severe symptoms.
Biological treatment
🧘
Mindfulness (MBCT)
Hover to learn more
How it works
Teaches patients to observe anxious thoughts without reacting — separating the person from the thought rather than fighting it.
Psychological treatment
⚡
Exposure Therapy (ERP)
Hover to learn more
How it works
Gradual, safe exposure to fear triggers without performing avoidance behaviours. Commonly used for phobias and OCD.
Behavioural treatment
🏃
Lifestyle Modifications
Hover to learn more
How it works
Regular exercise reduces cortisol, while good sleep hygiene, reduced caffeine, and social connection all clinically lower anxiety levels.
